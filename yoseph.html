Yoseph Shibiru

In love with learning

Ever since I was a child, I was eager to learn about the natural order of my surroundings: how water transformed into electricity, why the length of the days throughout the year was not the same, or how flies could happily roam on a 25 Km/hr moving bus. Being raised in a small town did not confine my imagination. My days were spent searching for stories about anything that passed my gaze and well...inventing my own to those I couldn't find one. My curiosity has led me to venture and branch out of my norm: from founding a space club in St. Raguel High school and meeting an astronaut, working as a salesman, and starting a freelance business. But over the years, my love and passion for learning and mastering new skills remained consistent.
