The Futuro House Archives & Other Collections

Our extensive personal collection of "Thing's Futuro" includes memorabilia, photos and other items from the late 60's and early 70's through the decades all the way up to contemporary items. We also have an extensive collection of "period" documents and other items in The Charles Cleworth Futuro House Archive. This page documents smaller Futuro archives and collections that are also in our possession be they physical and in our collection or digital only collections.
